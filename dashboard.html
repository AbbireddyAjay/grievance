About Our Grievance System

Our grievance system is designed to provide students and staff with a streamlined platform to report issues and concerns. We believe in creating a transparent environment where problems are addressed promptly and effectively.

The system allows you to submit complaints across various categories including academic issues, administrative concerns, harassment incidents, infrastructure problems, and examination-related matters. Once submitted, you can track the progress of your complaints until they are resolved.

We are committed to ensuring that every voice is heard and every concern is addressed with the utmost care and consideration.
