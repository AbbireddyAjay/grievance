About the Grievance System

Welcome to our Student-Faculty Grievance System, a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the process of addressing concerns and issues within our academic community. This system provides a structured and transparent way for students and faculty members to register complaints, track their progress, and receive timely resolutions.


Our goal is to create an environment where every voice is heard and every concern is addressed professionally and efficiently. The system categorizes complaints into various domains such as academic matters, administrative issues, infrastructure concerns, and more, ensuring that each complaint reaches the appropriate department for swift resolution.


We believe in fostering a culture of accountability, respect, and continuous improvement. Your feedback helps us enhance the quality of education and services we provide.
